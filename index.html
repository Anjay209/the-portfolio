About me

Hi!
My name is Anjay and I have a deep desire for art. Ever since my first art class, the experience of making something so tangible with just a pen and paper inspired me to draw more and more. Because to create art is to create another world of your own design. Over time, pencils became pens, which transformed into watercolors and colored pencils, splattered onto a sheet of paper. Throughout my early school years, I had entered many school wide art competitions to challenge my art intellect. They brought forward angles I had never seen; things I had never drawn, ideas that I had never imagined. And through the very same pen and paper that I had sat in front of many times before, I still remember my young self, drawing my first line on a blank sheet of paper. The day my passion sparked, the day that I created, and begun a journey.
